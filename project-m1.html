Overview

Designed and commissioned the electrical & electronics hardware and control systems for a modular automated production line made up of three stations:
(A) Dispensing System,
(B) Rearing (combining biological processing with AI-based selection), and
(C) Capping of the AI-chosen outputs.
My main focus was electronics and controls—modular control panels, sensor/actuator integration, stepper drives, and VFD-controlled conveyors—to ensure reliable sequencing and synchronized material transfer across the line. With my mechanical background, I also supported with CAD/CAM, rapid prototyping and hands-on assembly during system integration to help the team deliver 10+ replicated lines with improved throughput, fewer transfer errors, and higher operational efficiency. Throughout commissioning, I followed site health and safety procedures, using safe isolation/lockout practices and risk-aware testing when working on equipment.

Highlights

  • Designed and commissioned electrical hardware and control panels for 10+ modular production lines.
  • Integrated 15+ sensors (IR, inductive) and stepper-motor driven mechanisms per unit for real-time actuation.
  • Commissioned VFD-controlled conveyors to synchronize material handling between stations A/B/C.
  • Reduced production transfer errors by 20% through improved sensing, sequencing, and conveyor tuning.
  • Contributed to a 23% increase in operational efficiency via higher uptime, repeatable commissioning, and stable station hand-offs.

Role & responsibilities

  • Owned electrical build and commissioning tasks across the modular line: control panels, field wiring, and I/O validation.
  • Commissioned stepper motor motion and conveyor hardware, aligning timing and interlocks across stations A/B/C.
  • Configured and tuned VFD conveyor parameters (speed, ramps, direction, interlocks) to stabilize material transfer.
  • Supported mechanical integration: CAD/CAM assistance, rapid prototyping, subsystem assembly, and on-bench fit checks.
  • Followed health & safety procedures during installation/testing, including safe isolation and risk-aware commissioning.

End result

  • Delivered a scalable, modular electrical/control architecture replicated across 10+ production lines.
  • Improved throughput and consistency by synchronizing conveyors and sensor-driven sequencing across stations.
  • Achieved measurable impact: 20% reduction in transfer errors and 23% increase in operational efficiency.
  • Executed dry tests and supported Factory Acceptance Test during commissioning, then created SOPs and delivered operator/technician training to ensure safe startup, running, and fault-recovery procedures
